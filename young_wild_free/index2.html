Our Mission 

Young, Wild, and Free focuses not only the wellbeing of children and sitters, but the parents who work so hard to ensure their children are safe and taken care of. We believe that all parents should get a break from time to time. Our childcare site will ensure that as the parent you remain, young, wild, and free. 

Plan a Getaway!

We know that being a parent is one of the hardest jobs. That's why we want to help to alleviate some of that stress, with all of our sitters being CPR and First Aid certified we can guarantee you a stress free experience. All of our babysitters are at least 18 years of age and have previous babysitting experience.
